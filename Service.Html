Our Services

Swimming Pool

Dive into luxury in our pristine outdoor swimming pool, surrounded by lush landscaping and comfortable loungers. For those seeking a more private experience, we also offer exclusive cabanas for rent. Welcome to our luxurious outdoor pool, the perfect oasis for relaxation and recreation. Surrounded by lush landscaping and comfortable lounge chairs, our pool offers a serene setting to unwind and soak up the sun. Dive into the crystal-clear waters for a refreshing swim, or simply relax poolside with a cocktail from our bar. Restaurant

Welcome to our exquisite restaurant, where culinary diversity meets exceptional taste. Serving a delightful array of local cuisines, Indian specialties, and Thai delicacies, our menu is crafted to satisfy every palate. Whether you're in the mood for a hearty traditional meal, a spicy Indian curry, or a flavorful Thai dish, we have something for everyone. Open daily from 5 AM to 12 PM, we cater to all your dining needs, offering breakfast, lunch, snacks, and dinner. Complement your meal with our selection of soft drinks and freshly brewed coffee. As you dine, enjoy the panoramic view of the river and mountains in the distance, providing a breathtaking backdrop. In the evening, the lights of the nearby city twinkle, adding a magical ambiance to your dining experience. We also feature live music performances every night by talented local artists, creating a lively and entertaining atmosphere. If you feel inspired, you are welcome to take the stage and sing your favorite tunes. Our welcoming atmosphere and attentive service create the perfect setting for a memorable meal. Join us and savor the flavors of our diverse culinary offerings while soaking in the stunning views and delightful entertainment.

Concierge

Our dedicated concierge team is here to ensure your stay is seamless and memorable, providing personalized assistance for every aspect of your visit.
