Welcome to Red Roof Resort

The place to forget your worries and enjoy your stay away from the chaos

Red Roof offers a unique blend of thrilling activities, cultural experiences, and comfortable accommodations. Here’s what you can expect: Wilderness Exploration: Nestled amidst lush forests, this hotel allows you to explore the wilderness of Sauraha. Whether it’s a jungle walk or a jeep safari, you’ll encounter exotic wildlife up close. Canoe Trips: Immerse yourself in the tranquil beauty of the Nepalese countryside with peaceful canoe trips along the Rapti River. Tharu Culture: Discover the rich heritage of the Tharu people by visiting a traditional Tharu village. Learn about their customs, traditions, and way of life. Bird Watching: Grab your binoculars and embark on a bird-watching adventure. Sauraha is home to over 500 species of birds, making it a paradise for bird enthusiasts. Local Cuisine: Indulge in authentic Nepalese flavors with locally sourced and freshly prepared dishes. The food here is a delightful blend of tradition and taste. Comfortable Accommodations: Relax and unwind in spacious hotel rooms equipped with modern amenities. After a day of adventure, you’ll appreciate the cozy ambiance. Vibrant Cultural Performances: Experience the energy of traditional Nepalese dance performances. Colorful costumes and lively music create an unforgettable atmosphere. Jungle Activities: Join wildlife safaris, birdwatching tours, and nature walks led by experienced guides. Get ready for thrilling encounters with nature. Kandel Bhatti is more than just a place to stay; it’s an immersive experience that connects you with the natural beauty and cultural richness of Nepal. Whether you’re an adventure seeker or a culture enthusiast, this hotel promises unforgettable moments.
