About Us

Red Roof

Welcome to Red Roof Resort, your haven of luxury and comfort in the heart of Chitwan. Our story began in 2008 as a family-run resort, with a vision to create a unique hospitality experience that combines the charm of our local culture with the highest standards of service. Nestled in the heart of Chitwan national park, our resort has been a cornerstone of elegance and sophistication since its inception. Our hotel boasts a rich history, where modern luxury meets timeless tradition. Over the years, we have welcomed countless guests from around the world, providing them with memorable experiences and exceptional service.

Our Mission

At Red Roof, our mission is to offer an unparalleled hospitality experience that exceeds our guests' expectations. We are dedicated to creating a warm and inviting atmosphere, where every guest feels valued and at home. Our team is committed to providing personalized service, ensuring that your stay with us is nothing short of extraordinary.
